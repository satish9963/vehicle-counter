🚀 GETTING STARTED NOW

Step 1: Run Setup
  Windows: setup.bat
  Mac/Linux: ./setup.sh

Step 2: Launch
  npm run full-dev

Step 3: Upload a Video
  - Click "Browse Files" or drag video
  - Set speed (1x-10x)
  - Click "Start Detection"
  - View results

Step 4: Export Report
  - Click "Export as CSV" or "Export as JSON"
  - Open in Excel or custom analysis tool
